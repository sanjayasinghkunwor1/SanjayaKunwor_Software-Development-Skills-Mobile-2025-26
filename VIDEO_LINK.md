## What the Video Shows

The demo video demonstrates all 7 professional features of the StudyTime app:

1. **Main Timer Screen** - Large circular Pomodoro timer (25 minutes)
2. **Timer Controls** - Start, Pause, Resume, and Reset functionality
3. **Session Counter** - Tracking Pomodoro cycles (1/4, 2/4, etc.)
4. **Session History** - ListView displaying 12 pre-loaded sample sessions
5. **Statistics Dashboard** - 4 key metrics with real-time calculations
6. **Settings** - Customization options and preferences
7. **Bottom Navigation** - Smooth navigation between all 4 main screens
